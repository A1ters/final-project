Wuhan One week tour:

Wuhan>Xiaogan>Jingzhou>Yichang>Enshi

more info

[Route]

D1 Wuhan: Goode Temple>Hankou River Beach>Hubei Provincial Museum>Donghu Tingtao Scenic Area>Wuhan University>Chuhe Han Street Take the high-speed rail to Wuhan, usually at Hankou Station, where you can stay nearby. Today's itinerary is mainly to explore the scenic spots near East Lake.

D2 Wuhan: Hubu Lane>Huanghe Tower>Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge>Qingchuan Pavilion>Jianghan Road Pedestrian Street Get up early and go to Hubu Lane for breakfast, check in at the landmark Huanghe Tower, visit the Yangtze River Bridge by the river, and then return to Jianghan District for shopping.

D3 Xiaogan: Dongyong Park>Jinhui Manor>Zhongbai Warehouse (Xiaogan Shopping Plaza Store) Take the high-speed train to Xiaogan, which takes about half an hour by car. Spend most of the day in Xiaogan and taste authentic Xiaogan rice wine.

D4 Jingzhou: Jingzhou Ancient City>Zhang Juzheng's Former Residence>Guandi Temple>Guanyu Temple>Jingzhou Museum It takes about 2 hours by car from Xiaogan to Jingzhou by high-speed rail. Explore the ancient city of Jingzhou, visit numerous historical attractions, and deeply experience the culture of the Three Kingdoms.

D5 Yichang: Xilingxia Scenic Area>Sanyou Cave>Gezhouba>Yiling Yangtze River Bridge Taking the high-speed rail to Yichang takes about 40 minutes by car. Go to Xiling Gorge for sightseeing and visit Gezhouba Shiplock

D6 Yichang: 185 Viewing Platform>Tanziling Scenic Spots>Three Gorges Dam Tourist Scenic Area - Interception Memorial Park Go on a one-day tour to the Three Gorges Dam tourist area and enjoy the magnificent scenery of the Three Gorges Dam.

D7 Enshi City: Enshi Tusi City>Enshi Tujia Daughter City

D8 Enshi City: Enshi Grand Canyon>Yunlong Ground Gap>Qixingzhai Scenic Area>One Burner of Incense Participate in a one-day tour of Enshi Grand Canyon. If you have plenty of time, you can also stay overnight in the Grand Canyon and enjoy the trip in two days. If you take the high-speed rail back to Wuhan later, it will take about 4 hours by car
